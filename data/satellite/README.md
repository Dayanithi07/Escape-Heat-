## Description
This category is designated for high-resolution Land Surface Temperature (LST) raster files derived from satellite thermal sensors:
- **USGS/NASA Landsat 8/9 (TIRS)**: 30m resolution, ideal for micro-scale urban heat island analysis.
- **Copernicus Sentinel-3 (SLSTR)**: 1km resolution, daily coverage, perfect for regional heat monitoring.
- **ISRO Bhuvan AWiFS / LST**: Regional high-resolution temperature maps for India.

## Intended Usage
Used by the Heat Intelligence Engine to generate high-resolution heat map layers and identify local urban heat islands (UHI) at a neighborhood level.
